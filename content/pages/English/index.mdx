The house is located in Cadine, Via della Posta 44, 8 minutes away from Trento by car and 14 minutes by bus. You can easily get to all the locations in the city centre and it is an optimal area, at just 2 minuts by foot there is a grocery store, bank, pizzeria, bar ed estetista. The location offers a nice place to study especially during the summer, quite and calm.
